Frequency band for the channel (2.4 / 5 / 6 GHz).

Use WIFI_FREQ_BAND_UNKNOWN to let the driver infer when unambiguous (e.g. 36-165 is 5 GHz). Required when channel is ambiguous (e.g. 1-14 can be 2.4 or 6 GHz).
