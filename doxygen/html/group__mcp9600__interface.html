◆ sensor_attribute_mcp9600

enum sensor_attribute_mcp9600

#include <zephyr/drivers/sensor/mcp9600.h>

Custom sensor attributes for MCP9600.

Enumerator
SENSOR_ATTR_MCP9600_ADC_RES 

ADC resolution.

  • sensor_value.val1 is the resolution value (see mcp9600_adc_resolution).
  • sensor_value.val2 is unused (always 0).
SENSOR_ATTR_MCP9600_FILTER_COEFFICIENT 

IIR filter coefficient (0-7).

  • sensor_value.val1 is the filter coefficient.
  • sensor_value.val2 is unused (always 0).
SENSOR_ATTR_MCP9600_THERMOCOUPLE_TYPE 

Thermocouple type.

  • sensor_value.val1 is the thermocouple type (see mcp9600_thermocouple_type).
  • sensor_value.val2 is unused (always 0).
SENSOR_ATTR_MCP9600_COLD_JUNCTION_RESOLUTION 

Cold junction temperature resolution.

  • sensor_value.val1 is the resolution value (see mcp9600_cold_junction_resolution).
  • sensor_value.val2 is unused (always 0).
SENSOR_ATTR_MCP9600_DEV_ID 

Device ID (read-only).

  • sensor_value.val1 is the 16-bit device ID (chip ID in high byte, revision in low byte).
  • sensor_value.val2 is unused (always 0).

◆ sensor_channel_mcp9600

enum sensor_channel_mcp9600

#include <zephyr/drivers/sensor/mcp9600.h>

Custom sensor channels for MCP9600.

Enumerator
SENSOR_CHAN_MCP9600_COLD_JUNCTION_TEMP 

Cold junction (reference) temperature in degrees Celsius.

  • sensor_value.val1 is the integer part of the temperature (°C).
  • sensor_value.val2 is the fractional part (in millionths of a degree).
SENSOR_CHAN_MCP9600_HOT_JUNCTION_TEMP 

Hot junction (thermocouple tip) temperature in degrees Celsius.

  • sensor_value.val1 is the integer part of the temperature (°C).
  • sensor_value.val2 is the fractional part (in millionths of a degree).
SENSOR_CHAN_MCP9600_DELTA_TEMP 

Temperature difference (hot junction minus cold junction) in degrees Celsius.

  • sensor_value.val1 is the integer part of the delta temperature (°C).
  • sensor_value.val2 is the fractional part (in millionths of a degree).
SENSOR_CHAN_MCP9600_RAW_ADC 

Raw ADC value (thermocouple EMF in ADC counts).

  • sensor_value.val1 is the raw 24-bit signed ADC value.
  • sensor_value.val2 is unused (always 0).
